Summary

Inception is a science fiction thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. The film follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who steals secrets from within the subconscious during the dream state. Cobb is offered a chance to have his criminal history erased as payment for a task considered to be impossible — "inception": planting an idea into someone’s mind.

  • Joseph Gordon-Levitt as Arthur
  • Elliot Page as Ariadne
